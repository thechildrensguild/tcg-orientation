About The Children's Guild

Who we are and what we do

Founded in 1953, The Children's Guild serves children, families, and communities through a network of schools, mental health programs, therapeutic foster care, and community-based services across Maryland and Washington, DC. Our approach is guided by our unique Transformation Education philosophy — the belief that every child can learn and succeed when given the right environment and support.

With 500+ team members working across multiple campuses and programs, we are one of Maryland's most established nonprofits dedicated to transforming lives through education and care.

We're glad you're joining us!

Your First Two Weeks

There are two tracks depending on your start date. Here's what to expect.

OJT-First Track

Week 1 at Your Site, Week 2 Orientation

Week 1: You report to your program site Monday through Friday for on-the-job training. Your site contact will have your schedule and help you get settled.

Week 2: Monday and Tuesday are orientation at a central location. Wednesday through Friday, you return to your program site.

Orientation-First Track

Week 1 Orientation, Then Your Site

Week 1: Monday and Tuesday are orientation at a central location. Wednesday through Friday, you report to your program site.

Week 2: You're at your program site all week, continuing on-the-job training.

Attendance Requirement: You are expected to attend all 10 days of your first two weeks if you are on the OJT-First track (5 days OJT + 2 days orientation + 3 days OJT). If you are on the Orientation-First track, you are expected to attend all 5 days of your first week (2 days orientation + 3 days on-the-job training).
Check your personal portal (sent to your email) for your exact schedule and dates. This page covers the general information that applies to everyone.

What to Expect on Orientation Day

Here's a look at how your day will flow.

🍽️
Food

Breakfast and lunch are provided on your first day of orientation (Monday of orientation week only — not during OJT week). Bring your own if you prefer. A refrigerator and microwave are available. Please notify Elizabeth Garcia if you have any food allergies or dietary restrictions.

🧥
Clothing

Business casual. Rooms can be cool, so bring layers or a jacket.

⏰
Timing

Please arrive by 8:50 AM. Check in at the front desk and we'll show you where to go.

❄️
Weather Delays

Your orientation location follows the local school district's weather delays and closings. We will also send you a text when we know if there is a delay or closing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

What should I wear?
Business casual. Rooms can be cool, so bring layers or a jacket.
What if there's bad weather?
Follow your local school district's delays and closings. We will text you if there's a change to the orientation schedule.
Can I bring my own lunch?
Yes! A refrigerator and microwave are available at all locations. Lunch is provided on orientation Monday.
When will I get my work email?
Typically within your first week. You'll receive a separate email with your credentials.
What if there are any issues?
If there are any issues, contact Elizabeth Garcia at 443-829-6155.
