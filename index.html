About The Children's Guild

Who we are and what we do

The Children's Guild is a nonprofit organization that transforms the lives of children, families, and communities through education and care. With 500+ team members across Maryland and Washington, DC, we operate schools, mental health programs, therapeutic foster care, and community services.

We're glad you're joining us!

Your First Two Weeks

There are two tracks depending on your start date. Here's what to expect.

Most Common

OJT-First Track

Your first week is on-the-job training at your program site. Your second week starts with two days of orientation at a central location, then you return to your site.

Alternate

Orientation-First Track

Your first week starts with two days of orientation at a central location, then you report to your program site.

Your personal portal (sent to your work email) has your exact schedule, dates, and directions. This page covers the general information that applies to everyone.

What to Bring

Make sure you have these items ready for orientation day.

  • 📄
    Original I-9 Documents Option A: One document from List A (e.g., valid U.S. passport or passport card, permanent resident card).
    Option B: One from List B (e.g., driver's license or state ID) and one from List C (e.g., Social Security card or birth certificate).
    Documents must be originals, not copies.
  • 🪧
    Photo ID A government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license or passport.
  • ✍️
    Something to Take Notes With A notebook and pen, or your phone/tablet if you prefer digital notes.
  • 🧣
    Layers or a Jacket Meeting rooms can be cool. Dress in layers so you stay comfortable.
  • 📱
    Your Phone You'll need it to access your personal portal and for some setup steps.

What to Expect on Orientation Day

Here's a look at how your day will flow.

  • Arrive by 8:50 AM

    Check in at the front desk. We'll show you where to go.

  • Breakfast and Lunch Provided

    We'll have breakfast ready when you arrive and lunch will be provided on Day 1.

  • Meet the Leadership Team

    You'll hear from leaders across the organization and learn about TCG's mission, values, and culture.

  • I-9 Document Verification

    HR will verify your original I-9 documents. Be sure to bring them with you.

  • Get Your Employee Badge

    We'll take your photo and issue your ID badge during orientation.

  • Topics Covered

    Benefits overview, policies and procedures, safety training, and The Children's Guild culture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

What should I wear?
Business casual. Comfortable shoes are recommended since you'll be moving between rooms throughout the day.
What if there's bad weather?
Follow your local school district's delays and closings. We will text you if there's a change to the orientation schedule.
Can I bring my own lunch?
Yes! A refrigerator and microwave are available. Lunch is also provided on Day 1.
When will I get my work email?
Typically within your first week. You'll receive a separate email with your credentials.
What if I'm running late?
Call Elizabeth Garcia at 443-829-6155 to let us know.
