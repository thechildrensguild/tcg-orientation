🏢About Your First Two Weeks

👥
Week 1: Job Training at Your Program Site

Your first week is spent at your actual work location. You will get to know your team, your space, and your day-to-day role. Your team leader will guide you through your first days. Dress code and hours follow your specific program site.

🏫
Week 2: Classroom Orientation

Your second week is classroom orientation at the Transformation Academy with all new team members starting that cycle. You will learn about the TCG mission, policies, and benefits and meet colleagues from across the organization.

❓
Questions Before You Start?

Reach out to your hiring manager or contact Elizabeth Garcia at garciae@childrensguild.org or 443-829-6155. We want you to feel fully prepared!

📄Required Documents for Day 1

⚠️
Your first day of employment is Day 1 at your job training site — the Monday before orientation week. Federal law requires I-9 verification within 3 business days. Bring your original documents on that first day.

Bring ORIGINAL documents (not copies). Human Resources will verify them on your first day.

A One Document

Bring ANY ONE of these:

✓ U.S. Passport (book or card)
✓ Green Card (Permanent Resident Card)
✓ Work Permit (EAD Card)
OR
B Two Documents

Bring ONE from EACH column:

📸 Photo ID
• Driver's License
• State ID Card
• School ID (with photo)
📋 Work Proof
• Social Security Card
• Birth Certificate (with seal)
